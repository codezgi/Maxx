AC Double Implant Oval

AC Double Implant Oval is a specialized medical implant system developed by Maxx Global Medical using advanced engineering technologies and high-precision manufacturing processes. It is designed to provide reliable fixation, balanced support, and superior performance in surgical implant applications. Thanks to its oval design, the implant offers enhanced anatomical compatibility and controlled placement, contributing to stable, secure, and dependable performance throughout surgical procedures.

Featuring a dual-implant structure that provides enhanced connection strength and support, AC Double Implant Oval is developed for long-term performance through its biocompatible material composition and durable design. Manufactured with exceptional precision using advanced CNC Swiss-type turning technologies, the system is produced in accordance with international quality standards and undergoes comprehensive quality control procedures. This ensures maximum accuracy, minimal error rates, and high operational efficiency.

Maxx Global Medical provides high-precision manufacturing solutions for the medical industry, operating with a production approach centered on quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

With its strong engineering infrastructure, experienced technical team, and innovative manufacturing approach, the company serves not only as a manufacturer but also as a trusted solution partner that adds value to every project. AC Double Implant Oval delivers professional and effective solutions for medical procedures through its durable construction, precision manufacturing quality, and reliable performance characteristics.

AC Double Implant Oval is also referred to as an acromioclavicular (AC) joint double-button implant.
