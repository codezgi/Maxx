Helix TI Screw Anchor 3.5mm Peek

Helix TI Screw Anchor 3.5mm Peek is a specialized medical implant system developed by Maxx Global Medical using advanced engineering technologies and high-precision manufacturing processes. It is designed to provide reliable performance, precise handling, and long-term durability in surgical fixation applications. With its compact 3.5 mm design, the implant offers excellent suitability for precision surgical procedures, ensuring stable and controlled performance throughout the operation.

Thanks to its PEEK material construction, Helix TI Screw Anchor 3.5mm Peek offers excellent biocompatibility, lightweight performance, and outstanding durability. Its helical screw design provides strong gripping strength and reliable fixation, ensuring dependable performance in surgical applications. Manufactured with exceptional precision using advanced CNC Swiss-type turning technologies, the system is developed in accordance with international quality standards and undergoes comprehensive quality control procedures.
