Helix Titanium TI Screw

Helix Titanium TI Screw is a specialized medical implant solution developed by Maxx Global Medical using advanced engineering technologies and high-precision manufacturing infrastructure. It is designed to provide strong fixation, reliable performance, and long-term durability in surgical applications. Thanks to its titanium material construction, the implant offers exceptional strength and biocompatibility, ensuring stable and controlled performance throughout surgical procedures.

Featuring a helical screw design that provides superior grip strength and secure fixation, the system delivers effective solutions for surgical applications through its precise placement capabilities. Manufactured with exceptional accuracy using advanced CNC Swiss-type turning technologies, Helix Titanium TI Screw is developed in accordance with international quality standards and undergoes comprehensive quality control procedures at every stage of production. This approach ensures maximum precision, minimal error rates, and high operational efficiency.

Maxx Global Medical provides high-precision manufacturing solutions for the medical industry, operating with a manufacturing philosophy based on quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

With its strong engineering infrastructure, experienced technical team, and innovative manufacturing approach, the company serves not only as a manufacturer but also as a trusted solution partner that adds value to every project. Helix Titanium TI Screw delivers professional and effective solutions for medical procedures through its durable titanium construction, precision manufacturing quality, and reliable performance characteristics.

Helix Titanium TI Screw is also referred to as a titanium interference screw.
