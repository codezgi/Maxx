TI-BUTTON Without Loop

TI-BUTTON Without Loop is a specialized system solution developed by Maxx Global Medical using advanced engineering technologies. It is designed to provide reliable fixation, high durability, and precise handling in medical applications. Thanks to its loop-free structure, the product offers versatile usability for different surgical requirements, standing out with its stable performance and dependable design.

Manufactured using high-quality biocompatible materials, TI-BUTTON Without Loop is precisely processed with advanced CNC Swiss-type turning technologies. Comprehensive quality control procedures are implemented at every stage of the production process to ensure maximum precision, minimal error rates, and long-term durability. Thanks to its robust structure and controlled application features, the product delivers reliable performance throughout surgical procedures.

Maxx Global Medical provides high-precision manufacturing solutions for the medical industry, operating with a production approach focused on quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

With its advanced production infrastructure, experienced technical team, and engineering-focused approach, the company serves not only as a manufacturer but also as a strong solution partner that adds value to every project. TI-BUTTON Without Loop delivers professional and effective solutions for medical procedures through its durable structure, precision manufacturing quality, and reliable usability characteristics.

TI-BUTTON Without Loop is also referred to as a loopless titanium cortical button in the literature.
