Titanium Peek Knotless Pushlock

Titanium PEEK Knotless Pushlock is an advanced engineering system developed by Maxx Global Medical using high-precision manufacturing technologies. It is designed to provide strong fixation, reliable performance, and long-term durability in medical applications. Thanks to its titanium and PEEK material combination, the product offers excellent strength and biocompatibility, ensuring stable and secure use in surgical procedures.

Thanks to its knotless design, the system enables effective fixation without the need for knots, simplifying application procedures while ensuring controlled and precise use during surgery. The Pushlock mechanism provides strong grip performance, delivering reliable fixation support. Manufactured with precision using advanced CNC Swiss-type turning technologies, the product is developed in compliance with high quality standards and undergoes comprehensive quality control procedures.

Maxx Global Medical provides advanced engineering solutions for the medical industry, combining quality, reliability, and sustainable manufacturing principles with a production approach tailored to customer needs.

With its experienced technical team, modern manufacturing infrastructure, and process-based quality control approach, the company operates not only as a manufacturer but also as a strong solution partner that adds value to every project. Titanium PEEK Knotless Pushlock delivers professional solutions for medical procedures through its high-precision manufacturing quality, durable material structure, and reliable performance characteristics.
